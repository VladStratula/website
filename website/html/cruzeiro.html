World champions Sada Cruzeiro take Superliga title again

Lausanne, Switzerland, April 10, 2016-Sada Cruzeiro won their fourth Brazilian Superliga title on Sunday, beating Brazil Kirin 3-1 (23-25, 25-23, 25-15, 30-28) at the Nilson Nelson Arena in Brasilia.

Marcelo Mendez's team captured their third consecutive title.

Cuban Leal was named the best player of the match after scoring 22 points.

attitude of his players: As a team, we've made history in Brazil and we are overjoyed. Our group has fought and always believed in the coaching staff and the Sada Cruzeiro project. The result of all this is the titles and victories we've had.

Brazil Kirin's Lucas Loh lamented the defeat: It leaves a bad taste in my mouth. Our team had quality and were united in trying to win this final. This was a first for us and we are proud of that. We will fight hard to achieve greater things. I hope we can follow in the footsteps of Sada.
