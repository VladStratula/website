Women's Qualification

As hosts, Brazil were the first country to qualify for the Olympics.

FIVB Volleyball Women's World Cup Japan 2015 winners China and runners-up Serbia will be joined by the five winners of each Continental Olympic Qualification Tournament.

The qualification tournament for Asian teams also doubles as a World Olympic Qualification Tournament and takes place in Japan between the periods of May 14 to June 5, 2016. The hosts are joined by the top three ranked teams from Asia according to the FIVB Women's world ranking as well as the second and third placed teams from the European Olympic Qualification Tournament, the second placed team from the South American Olympic Qualification Tournament and the second placed team from the NORCECA Olympic Qualification Tournament. The best ranked Asian team plus the top three ranked teams will qualify for the Olympics. If an Asian team is ranked among the top three, then the next ranked team will be qualified.

The final vacancy is decided at a second World Qualification Tournament due to be held in the period from May 14 to June 5, 2016. The third placed team from the South American Olympic Qualification Tournament, the third placed team from the NORCECA Olympic Qualification Tournament and the second and third placed teams from the African Olympic Qualification Tournament compete for the final berth.
